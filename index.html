Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

Data Collection

HobbyMax does not collect any personal information or usage data. All data is stored locally on your device using SwiftData.

Data Storage

All information you enter into the app, such as books and reading logs, is stored directly on your device. No information is sent to our servers or third-party services.

Future Features

Future versions of HobbyMax may offer optional syncing with a local backend service that you set up yourself. If we implement any syncing or online features that may collect personal data, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

Your Rights

You retain full control of the data on your device. You can delete the app or its data at any time.
